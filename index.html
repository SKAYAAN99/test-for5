Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking a repair with CoolComfort

For standard bookings, we typically dispatch within 2–4 hours. Book before noon and you can often get same-day service. Our Emergency slot (available 24/7) guarantees a technician within 90 minutes with a nominal ₹200 surcharge.

Yes — every repair includes a 90-day service warranty. If the same issue recurs within 90 days, we fix it at no additional charge. Spare parts also carry their own manufacturer warranty, typically 6–12 months depending on the component.

We service all major brands — Samsung, LG, Whirlpool, Daikin, Voltas, Haier, Bosch, Godrej, IFB, Panasonic, Blue Star, Hitachi, Toshiba, Sony, and many more. If you don't see your brand listed, just call us — we most likely service it.

After booking, you'll receive a unique Booking ID via SMS. Enter this ID with your phone number in the "Track Your Repair" section above to see real-time updates — from Pending all the way to Completed. You'll also receive automated SMS alerts at each stage.

We accept all major payment methods: UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), debit/credit cards, net banking, and cash. Annual Maintenance Packs also support 3-month EMI at zero interest. Payment is collected only after the repair is complete and you're satisfied.

Absolutely. Every technician undergoes comprehensive background verification, identity checks, and manufacturer-level skills certification before joining our team. They carry photo ID badges, and you'll receive the technician's name and photo via SMS before they arrive at your home.

There is a nominal ₹99 diagnosis visit fee to cover the technician's travel costs. However, this amount is fully waived if you proceed with the repair. New customers using the "First Service Free" offer also have this fee waived entirely.
